Organizer Guide - App Distribution to Students
Dual Job Dating  |  2026
Scope. This document covers everything the organizer must do to get students onto the Dual Job Dating app. It assumes student accounts have already been created in the backend and that each student has received their login credentials (email address and password) from the organizer. Account provisioning is out of scope.

Overview

The Dual Job Dating app allows students to browse participating companies and vote on them (Like, Dislike, or Neutral). After the organizer runs the matching process in the backend, each student can view their assigned appointments in the app.

The app is available on Android and iOS. Each platform uses a different distribution channel described in the sections below.

Android Distribution

Google Play - Closed Testing
1

Send the Google Group link to Android students

Students must join the closed testing group before they can install the app. Send every Android student the following link and ask them to sign in with their Google account and join the group:

https://groups.google.com/g/dualjobdating

It can take a few minutes after joining for the Play Store to grant access. If the app is not visible immediately, ask the student to wait and refresh.

2

Send the setup guide PDF and login credentials

Along with the Google Group link, provide each student with their email address and password, and attach the relevant setup guide PDF:

  • How_to_Get_the_App.pdf  (English)
  • So_bekommst_du_die_App.pdf  (Deutsch)

The PDF guides the student step-by-step through joining the group, finding the app in the Play Store, and installing it.

3

Student installs the app from the Play Store

Once the student has joined the group and the Play Store has synced, they search for Dual Job Dating in the Play Store and install the app. The PDF includes the direct Play Store link.

4

Student logs in and votes on companies

On first launch the student enters their email address and password. After a successful login they see the company voting screen. They swipe or tap to vote Like, Dislike, or Neutral on each company. Votes are submitted to the backend in real time. A confirmation screen appears when all companies have been voted on.

iOS Distribution

TestFlight
Incomplete - action required. The iOS TestFlight setup is managed by a separate team member. The steps below are structurally correct but the TestFlight invite link and the iOS setup guide PDF are placeholders. These must be filled in before this document is shared with students.
1

Student installs TestFlight

TestFlight is Apple's official beta distribution platform and must be installed before the student can install Dual Job Dating. The student searches for TestFlight in the App Store, or uses the link below, and installs it for free:

https://apps.apple.com/app/testflight/id899247664
2

Send the TestFlight invite link to iOS students

Share the TestFlight public link for the Dual Job Dating beta with all iOS students. The student opens the link on their device, which launches TestFlight and allows them to install the app directly.

4

Student installs the app via TestFlight

After opening the invite link on their device, the student taps Accept in TestFlight and then Install. Dual Job Dating is installed on the device.

5

Student logs in and votes on companies

Identical to Android - on first launch the student enters their email address and password. After login they see the company voting screen and can vote on each company.

After the Matching Process (Both Platforms)

Once the organizer has run the matching algorithm in the backend, each student's assigned appointments automatically appear in the Appointments tab of the app - on both Android and iOS. The student sees the company name and time slot for each appointment. No further action is required from the organizer on the app side.
