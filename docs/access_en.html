How to Get the App

The app is distributed via Google Play as a closed test. To install it you need a Google account and must follow these steps with that same account throughout.

Use the same Google account for the group and the Play Store. If they differ, the store page will not grant you access.

Step 1 - Join the Google Group

Open the Google Group link you received from the event organizer. Make sure you are logged in with your Google account, then click Join group.

Step 2 - Confirm

A dialog appears asking for your display name and subscription settings. Click Join group to confirm.

Step 3 - Install the App

Once you are a member, open the App Download post in the group and click the Play Store link.

On the Play Store page, click Join the testing program if prompted. After that, click Install.
