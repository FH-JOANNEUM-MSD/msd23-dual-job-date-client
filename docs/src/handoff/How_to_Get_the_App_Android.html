How to Get the App
Setup Guide for Android Students
Dual Job Dating  |  Sieff & Winkler  |  2026
What you'll need. An Android device running Android 7.0 or higher, a Google account, your login credentials (email and password) from the organizer, and a working internet connection. Use the same Google account for the group and the Play Store - otherwise the store will not grant you access.

Overview

The Dual Job Dating app for Android is distributed through Google Play as a closed test. You first join a Google Group with your Google account, which grants you access to the app in the Play Store. After installing the app you log in with the credentials your organizer sent and start voting on companies.

Installation

Android - Google Play
1

Join the Google Group

Open the Google Group link below. Make sure you are signed in with your Google account, then tap Join group:

https://groups.google.com/g/dualjobdating
2

Confirm

A dialog appears asking for your display name and subscription settings. Tap Join group to confirm.

3

Open the App Download post

Once you are a member, open the App Download post in the group and tap the Play Store link.

It can take a few minutes after joining for the Play Store to recognise your access. If the app page does not open immediately, wait a moment and try again.

4

Install the app

On the Play Store page, tap Join the testing program if prompted. Then tap Install. The app is installed on your device like any other Play Store app.

5

Open the app and log in

Tap the Dual Job Dating icon on your home screen. On the login screen, enter the email address and password your organizer sent you and tap Sign in. After successful login you'll see the company voting screen.

6

Vote on the companies

Swipe each company's card right to Like, left to Dislike, or up to mark Neutral. You can also tap the buttons under the card. When you're done with all companies, a confirmation screen appears.

After voting - your appointments

Once the organizer has run the matching process, your assigned appointments appear automatically in the Appointments tab inside the app. You'll see which company you'll meet, at what time, and where. No further action is required from you - just open the app on the day of the event.

Troubleshooting

The Play Store says you don't have access. Make sure you are signed into the Play Store with the same Google account you used to join the group. The two accounts must match.

The app does not appear in the Play Store immediately. It can take a few minutes for access to propagate. Wait, then refresh the Play Store.

Login fails. Double-check the email and password your organizer sent. Passwords are case-sensitive. If it still fails, contact your organizer.
