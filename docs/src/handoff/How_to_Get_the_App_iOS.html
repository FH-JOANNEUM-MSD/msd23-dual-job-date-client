How to Get the App
Setup Guide for iOS Students
Dual Job Dating  |  Sieff & Winkler  |  2026
What you'll need. An iPhone or iPad running iOS 16 or higher, your login credentials (email and password) from the organizer, and a working internet connection. The whole setup takes about 5 minutes.

Overview

The Dual Job Dating app for iOS is distributed via TestFlight - Apple's official platform for beta apps. You'll first install TestFlight from the App Store, then use a special invite link to install Dual Job Dating itself. Once installed, you log in with the credentials your organizer sent you and start voting on companies.

Installation

iOS - TestFlight
1

Install TestFlight from the App Store

Open the App Store on your iPhone or iPad, search for TestFlight, and install it. It's free and made by Apple. You can also tap the link below on your device:

https://apps.apple.com/app/testflight/id899247664
2

Open the Dual Job Dating invite link

Open the following link on your iPhone or iPad (not on your computer). It will launch TestFlight and show the Dual Job Dating app:

https://testflight.apple.com/join/eJBwfHqd

If the link opens in a browser instead of TestFlight, make sure TestFlight is installed (step 1) and try again. Tapping the link from the Mail app or a messaging app usually works best.

3

Accept and install

In TestFlight, tap Accept to join the beta. Then tap Install. The app is downloaded and added to your home screen like any other app.

4

Open the app and log in

Tap the Dual Job Dating icon on your home screen. On the login screen, enter the email address and password your organizer sent you and tap Sign in. After successful login you'll see the company voting screen.

5

Vote on the companies

Swipe each company's card right to Like, left to Dislike, or up to mark Neutral. You can also tap the buttons under the card. When you're done with all companies, a confirmation screen appears.

After voting - your appointments

Once the organizer has run the matching process, your assigned appointments appear automatically in the Appointments tab inside the app. You'll see which company you'll meet, at what time, and where. No further action is required from you - just open the app on the day of the event.

Troubleshooting

The invite link won't open in TestFlight. Make sure TestFlight is installed first. Open the link from Mail or Messages on the iOS device itself, not from a desktop browser.

"This beta is full" or similar message. Contact your organizer - the public link may need to be refreshed.

Login fails. Double-check the email and password your organizer sent. Passwords are case-sensitive. If it still fails, contact your organizer.

App won't open after install. Try restarting your device. If the icon is missing from the home screen, open TestFlight and tap "Open" next to Dual Job Dating.
